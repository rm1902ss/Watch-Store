ABOUT

Watches of Naples is one of the most highly trusted luxury watch retailers located in the heart of the Downtown Naples in Southwest Florida. We sell an incredible selection of hard to find timepieces such as Richard Mille, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, and Rolex. We treat our customers like family and aim to build long-lasting relationships. Our mission is to give you the most memorable and stress-free shopping experience.

Our Private Upscale Showroom is modern, sleek, and trendy. You will be blown away with our elegant aesthetic and attention to detail. Through personalized and private viewings, we've helped thousands of customers choose the watch of their dreams. We ease the pressure of buying a watch by allowing each client to unwind with a cup of coffee or a glass of Champagne and making them feel right at home.
